The purpose of the Company is to act as a **holding and parent company**, including but not limited to:

* Owning, operating, controlling, and managing subsidiary entities
* Intellectual property ownership and licensing
* Asset protection and risk segregation
* Revenue collection, allocation, and intercompany services

### 2.2 Subsidiaries

The Company is the sole parent entity of the following subsidiaries:

1. **CharlesDub Audio** — a record label and music-related enterprise
2. **LL Rain Collections** — an adult entertainment and digital content enterprise

Each subsidiary shall operate as a legally distinct entity with separate books, accounts, contracts, and liabilities.

### 2.3 Powers

The Company shall have all powers permitted to an LLC under state law, including but not limited to:

* Acquiring and disposing of assets
* Entering contracts
* Licensing intellectual property
* Opening bank accounts
* Borrowing and lending funds
* Creating additional subsidiaries

## ARTICLE IV — MANAGEMENT

### 4.1 Management Structure

The Company shall be **Manager-Managed**.

* **Manager**: Dakota Watashe aka **[Charles Dubalaney]**

The Manager shall have full and exclusive authority to operate the Company, including control over subsidiaries.

### 4.3 Authority of Manager

The Manager may:

* Appoint officers or agents

## ARTICLE V — SUBSIDIARY GOVERNANCE & FIREWALLS

### 5.1 Legal Separation

Each subsidiary shall:

* Maintain separate EINs
* Maintain separate bank accounts
* Maintain separate accounting
* Enter its own contracts

### 5.2 Liability Shield

No debts, lawsuits, or liabilities of any subsidiary shall attach to O.W.L.S. LLC or to any other subsidiary.

### 5.3 Intercompany Agreements

All services, IP licensing, loans, or cost-sharing arrangements between the Company and subsidiaries must be documented via written intercompany agreements.

---

## ARTICLE VI — INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 6.1 Ownership

All intellectual property created by or acquired for the benefit of the Company or its subsidiaries shall be owned by **O.W.L.S. LLC**, unless explicitly assigned otherwise.

### 6.2 Licensing

Subsidiaries operate under revocable, non-transferable licenses granted by the Company.

### 6.3 Adult Content Safeguard

Adult entertainment IP and operations shall be strictly confined to **LL Rain Collections**, and shall not contaminate or legally attach to CharlesDub Audio or other subsidiaries.

## ARTICLE VII — FINANCES & DISTRIBUTIONS

### 7.1 Fiscal Year

The fiscal year shall be determined by the Manager.

### 7.2 Banking

All Company funds shall be deposited into accounts in the Company’s name.

### 7.3 Distributions

Distributions shall be made at the discretion of the Manager, subject to solvency requirements.

### 7.4 Intercompany Revenue Flow

Subsidiaries may remit profits to the Company via dividends, management fees, or royalties as structured.

## ARTICLE VIII — TAXATION

### 8.1 Tax Classification
